## 🚀 Featured Projects

### 📈 TradelyBox
*An end-to-end quantitative trading infrastructure.*
* **Architecture:** Combines a high-performance backtesting engine with a modern frontend (strategy builder).
* **AI Integration:** Leverages AI (including Claude Max) for automated strategy generation, performance analytics, and uninterrupted workflow debugging on complex data pipelines.
* **Capabilities:** Multi-asset support (forex, crypto, equities) executing short-term, ML-driven, and high-frequency trading logic.

### 🧮 Quantitative Research & Modeling
* **Heston Model Calibration:** Compared classic optimization, Fast Fourier Transform (FFT), and Deep Learning to calibrate option pricing on the SPX implied volatility surface (reducing compute from 2650s to 0.12s).
* **Options Pricing Engines:** Developed interactive web apps (Next.js/FastAPI) to price European and American options using Black-Scholes, Monte Carlo, and Binomial/Trinomial trees.
* **Interest Rate Swaps:** Implemented and calibrated Vasicek and CIR short-rate models from market curves.
* **Portfolio Optimization:** Built out-of-sample backtests for Mean-Variance, Risk Parity, and Black-Litterman models to evaluate Sharpe ratio, max drawdown, and stability.

### 🤖 FinBERT Market Sentiment Analysis
* Implemented a financial NLP pipeline processing real-time data from Twitter, Reddit, and Finviz to generate short-term trading signals based on market sentiment.
